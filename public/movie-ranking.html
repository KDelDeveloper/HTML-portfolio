The Best Movies According To Shannon Kate

My Top Favorite Movies Of All Time

Ocean's Eleven

The classic with George Clooney. It's able to be watched over and again. I love the wit and the character interaction.

Finding Forrester

Heartbreaking and charming. Sean Connery at his best. Two people of dissimilar origin brought together by the love of writing.

Last Holiday

Existentially funny. What would you do if you only had three weeks to live?
